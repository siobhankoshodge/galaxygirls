About the Club

Our Story & Values

Linslade Galaxy Girls FC is a community-focused football club dedicated to inspiring girls through sport. We provide a safe, welcoming, and competitive environment where every player can develop their skills and passion for the beautiful game.

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Linslade Galaxy Girls FC has grown from a small group of enthusiastic young players into one of Bedfordshire's most respected girls-only football clubs. We are proudly affiliated with the Football Association and compete in local and regional leagues.

Our club is built on the belief that every girl deserves the opportunity to play football in a safe, supportive, and fun environment. From our youngest U7 players taking their first kicks, to our senior U16s competing at a high level — Galaxy is for every girl.

We are run entirely by volunteers — dedicated parents, coaches, and committee members who give their time because they love seeing girls thrive through sport.

FA Affiliation & Accreditations

Linslade Galaxy Girls FC is proud to be affiliated with the Football Association, the governing body of football in England. This affiliation ensures that we meet the highest standards of player development, safeguarding, and club governance.

We are also registered members of the Beds & Herts Football Association, which oversees our participation in regional competitions and provides support for our coaching and development programmes.

Our commitment to excellence extends to our participation in the FA's Respect Campaign, which promotes positive behaviour and fair play across all levels of youth football.
