Common Questions

How do I register my daughter? +

To register your daughter, please complete the registration form available on our Documents page, or contact the club secretary at secretary@linsladegalaxygirlsfc.com. They will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have about joining our club.

What age groups do you cater for? +

We currently run teams from U7s through to U16s. Each age group has dedicated coaches and a training program tailored to their developmental stage. Please contact our registration team if you'd like to know more about any specific age group.

What kit does my daughter need? +

Club kit (shirt, shorts, and socks) is available to purchase from the club. Players will also need appropriate football boots and shin pads for safety during matches and training. We can provide information about where to source these items and typical costs involved.

How much are subscriptions? +

Subscription fees vary by age group. Please contact our treasurer or the registration team at registrations@linsladegalaxygirlsfc.com for the current season's fees and any available financial assistance options.
