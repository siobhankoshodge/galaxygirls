Welcome to the Galaxy

Linslade Galaxy Girls FC is a proud FA-affiliated girls football club based in Linslade, Bedfordshire. Since our founding in 2010, we've been dedicated to providing an inclusive, supportive, and competitive environment where girls can develop their football skills and build lasting friendships.

We welcome girls from U7 to U16 across our eight competitive teams. Whether your daughter is just starting her football journey or looking to compete at a higher level, Galaxy Girls has the perfect team for her. Our FA-qualified coaches are passionate about helping every player reach her full potential.
