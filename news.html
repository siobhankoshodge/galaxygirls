Galaxy U12s Secure Top of the Table with Dominant 4-0 Victory

In a thrilling display of attacking football, our U12 squad delivered a commanding performance to secure their position at the top of the league table with a convincing 4-0 victory over a spirited Ampthill side.

The team showed excellent discipline and coordination throughout the match, with clinical finishing and solid defensive work creating a foundation for their success. Goals from multiple players showcased the depth of talent within our squad.

This victory reinforces our commitment to developing young footballers in a supportive and competitive environment. Our coaching staff are delighted with the team's progress and development this season.
