Meet Our Teams

From our youngest beginners to our most experienced players, every girl has a team at Galaxy. We currently run 8 age-group teams across Bedfordshire.

We develop players, not just footballers.

At Linslade Galaxy Girls FC, we believe that youth football is about far more than just learning to kick a ball. Our FA-qualified coaches are committed to developing the whole player—building confidence, resilience, teamwork, and a lifelong love of the game.

Every girl who joins Galaxy has access to professional coaching with a long-term player development plan. From mini-football foundations to competitive league play, we create a pathway where every player can thrive at their level.

Our coaches put fun first. We know that girls who enjoy football stay in the sport, and that's why every training session is designed to be engaging, inclusive, and motivating.
